Step 4: Make it a kids workshop

This page is like my planning page. It helps turn the website into a 30–45 minute session for younger kids.

1. Warm-up

Ask kids: “What does your brain help you do?”

5 min

2. Explore

Open the brain map and let kids guess each lobe.

10 min

3. Create

Draw a brain superhero where each lobe has a power.

15 min

4. Quiz

Use the quiz as a fun ending, not a test.

5 min

Student note

I do not want this to feel like a lecture. I want kids to touch, draw, guess, laugh, and remember one or two things when they go home.

Materials

Paper, crayons, sticky notes, timer, and this website on a laptop or projector.
