Step 2: Weird but true brain facts

These are short on purpose because kids remember better when facts feel like “wait, really?” moments.

🧠 The human brain weighs about 3 pounds / 1.4 kg.
⚡ Your brain uses about 20% of your body’s oxygen.
🚀 Brain signals can travel very fast — up to around 268 mph.
💧 The brain is mostly water, so hydration matters.
🛌 Sleep helps your brain store memories.
👀 The occipital lobe helps process what you see.
🧩 Your brain sends messages through special cells called neurons.

Try this with younger kids

Ask: “Which fact surprised you the most?” Then let them draw that fact as a tiny cartoon.

Extra trivia

  • The brain itself does not feel pain the way skin does.
  • Your brain keeps changing as you learn new things.
  • Practice can strengthen brain pathways, like making a trail clearer.
